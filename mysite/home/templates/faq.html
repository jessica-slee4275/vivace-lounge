SMP is a non-invasive, semi-permanent tattooing technique that replicates the appearance of hair follicles on the scalp. It is suitable for covering thinning areas, bald spots, and creating a fuller hairline.

    Procedure Overview:
  • Uses micro-needles to deposit pigment into the upper dermis.
  • Customization based on skin tone, hair type, and desired look.
    Number of Sessions:
  • Typically requires 3–7 sessions.
  • Sessions are spaced 1 week apart for optimal results.
    Expected Results:
  • A natural, full-looking hairline and scalp appearance.
    Immediate Visual Results:
  • Noticeable improvement after each session.
