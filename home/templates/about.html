About Us

Vivace Lounge

At Vivace Lounge, we specialize in Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP), Brow Enhancements, and Skin Treatments, offering high-quality, customized solutions to help you achieve a natural and confident look. Our lead specialist holds an Anatomy Specialization from the University of Michigan and has received professional training from top SMP, Brow, and Skin Treatment Studios worldwide. With years of industry experience, we ensure the highest level of precision, artistry, and outstanding results for every client.

Our Philosophy

We believe in enhancing natural beauty through advanced techniques, premium products, and a client-focused approach. Our treatments are designed to be subtle yet impactful, ensuring that you leave our lounge feeling confident and rejuvenated.
